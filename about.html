About Azhari Academy

Authentic Islamic education, inspired by Al-Azhar, delivered to students worldwide.

At Azhari Academy, we believe that learning the Quran should be clear, structured, and accessible for everyone — regardless of age or background.

Our Foundation

How We Started

Our academy was established to provide authentic and high-quality Quran and Arabic education to students around the world through qualified and experienced teachers.

We bring together a team of specialised teachers in Quran recitation, Tajweed, and Arabic language. Our approach is inspired by the methodology of Al-Azhar — combining strong traditional foundations with simple and effective modern teaching methods.

Est. 2018  •  Trusted by Students Worldwide

Quran & Tajweed Specialists

Our teachers are specialists in Quran recitation and Tajweed, trained in the authentic Al-Azhar tradition. Every teacher brings clarity, patience, and deep knowledge to every lesson.

Advanced Qira'at Teachers

Among our team are teachers who have studied the Ten Qira'at and hold authentic Ijazah chains of transmission — some of the most qualified Quran teachers available online.

Arabic Language Experts

Our Arabic specialists cover all levels — from the Arabic alphabet and Quranic Arabic to conversational fluency and advanced grammar — using structured, engaging methods.

Our Students

A Journey for Every Age

We have worked with students from all age groups — from young children as early as 4 years old to learners in their 70s and 80s — supporting each student with care, consistency, and personal attention.

Over the years, we have helped more than 1,000 students from different countries start, improve, and excel in their Quran and Arabic journey. By the grace of Allah, many of our students have gone on to become teachers, imams, and active contributors in their communities.

At Azhari Academy, we do not only teach — we guide, support, and help build a lasting connection between the student and the Quran.
